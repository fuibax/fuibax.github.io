Alchemists of the Tower

Alchemists are skilled in physical and chemical magic, particularly so in the building and animation of golems. Hero portraits

This gallery contains portraits of heroes from Heroes of Might and Magic 3 (1999). The portraits have been upscaled from 64*58 pixel originals with Stable Diffusion, preserving some qualities and reimagining others. The portraits have been shared for the sole purposes of appreciation and fun. Original portraits can be seen here.

Heroes of the Refuge faction are novel and do not exist in the game.
